Our Story

The story of La Bijouterie began more than 20 years ago when founder Set Fournouzian moved to Africa in the early 1990's to learn everything there is to know about diamonds. After spending 10 years in Africa, Set decided that it was time to apply his knowledge in trading and polishing rough diamonds in the city known as the diamond capital of the world, Antwerp, Belgium.

During the next 10 years in Antwerp, Set became one of the most respected diamond traders in the business. Although he achieved great success, something was missing: he always dreamed of opening his own jewelry boutique to pass his love and passion of unique diamonds and jewelry to the masses. Set finally decided to fulfill his dream, and he and his beautiful wife picked the most beautiful city in the world, San Francisco, to open their first boutique, La Bijouterie.

La Bijouterie's loose diamond inventory is carefully and individually selected from different polishing factories around the world. The relationships that Set has with these diamond factories as well as the store's low overhead allow La Bijouterie to offer the most beautiful diamonds at the most competitive prices. In addition to diamonds, La Bijouterie also has custom jewelry manufacturing, using 3D jewelry designs and images to create anything that your creative juices come up with.

Come and visit La Bijouterie at 1931 Union Street in San Francisco to experience excellence in traditional and colored diamonds, exquisite gems, precious metals, and one-of-a-kind pearls!

Key Facts about La Bijouterie:

  • Our design philosophy is simple. Focus on beauty and craft with an abiding concern for customers.
  • Our GIA certified diamonds are laser engraved to assure proof positive identification, and come with printed laboratory certification.
  • We stand firmly against conflict diamonds and gold, unfair labor practices, and environmentally damaging mining.
  • We are a socially responsible business that is deeply committed to preserving the environment and supporting human rights.
  • As designers and craftspeople, we believe the human element is essential to exceptional design.
  • Jewelry must be thoughtfully designed and meticulously crafted using the finest materials that are obtained responsibly from partners who share our commitment to the global community and to the environment.
