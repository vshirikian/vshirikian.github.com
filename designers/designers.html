Designers

To complement our custom made jewelry in our stores, we also carry exquisite jewelry pieces from some of the top designers in the world. Here are a few of the designers we feature in our store along with a sample of their collections.


Simon G

Simon G is world renowned for artisan jewelry, focusing on engagement fashion jewelry and rings. A family run business, Simon G prides itself on creating new pieces everyday, whether it is a custom made wedding ring or a multi-colored stone bracelet.

Royal Asscher

The name Royal Asscher is synonymous with the finest and most prestigeous diamonds in the world. The company is still owned by the Asscher family, a renowned international diamond dynasty with an unparalleled 157 year old legacy of diamond innovation.

Pamela Froman

The award-winning Pamela Froman Collection features handmade jewelry pieces made with multiple precious stones and metals. Originality and a high attention to detail are paid to every design...a trademark of Pamela's signature look.

Tycoon

TYCOON has exemplified the excellence in craftsmanship through the years, resulting in the internationally known TYCOON CUT diamond. Their creativity, talent, honesty and integrity built their reputation amongst their peers and customers.
